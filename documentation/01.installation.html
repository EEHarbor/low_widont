--- title: Installation ---

Compatibility

Low Widon’t is compatible with EE3 and up. The EE1 and EE2 compatible version are no longer under development, but are still available on Github.

Installation

  • Download and unzip Low Widon’t
  • Copy the low_widont folder to your /system/user/addons/ directory
  • In your Control Panel, go to the Add-on Manager and install Low Widon’t.
  • All set!
